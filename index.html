Introduction

An upcoming superhero movie called Avengers: Secret Wars is based on the same-named superhero team from Marvel Comics. Following The Avengers, Avengers: Age of Ultron, Avengers: Infinity War, Avengers: Endgame, and Avengers: The Kang Dynasty, the movie is a prequel. It is the 42nd movie in the Marvel Cinematic Universe and the climactic chapter of The Multiverse Saga and Phase 6. The movie is scheduled to premiere on May 1st, 2026.
